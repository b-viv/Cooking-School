Course Introduction

Life in the Cooking School

In our cooking school, every day is another culinary adventure. The day starts early when fresh ingredients arrive from local producers and suppliers. Students and faculty work together to select the best vegetables, meats, and spices to use that day.

Before the lessons, the students prepare, prepare the tools and ingredients, while the instructors explain the daily schedule and the details of the dishes to be prepared. The kitchen is bustling with life as students chop, slice, roast and cook while constantly learning and improving.

The morning hours are often spent practicing basic techniques: how to use knives correctly, what techniques can be used to achieve the best results in different cooking methods, such as steaming, baking or grilling. The instructors help the students with personalized advice so that everyone can develop at their own pace.

During lunchtime, they taste the prepared dishes together, evaluate the flavors and discuss the experiences. This is a time of relaxation and community building, when students and faculty can enjoy meals prepared together.

Afternoon classes are often thematic: international cuisines, special diets or confectionary workshops. Instructors demonstrate special techniques and recipes, and students can try new challenges.

At the end of the day, everyone participates in cleaning and tidying up, so that the kitchen is ready for new adventures the next day. Students and instructors return home tired but satisfied, as every day they take another step on the path to becoming master chefs.

Everyday life at our cooking school is about learning, passion and community, where everyone is happy to share their knowledge and love for cooking.
